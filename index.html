How points work
10 pts for 1st boat, 9 for 2nd … 2 pts for 9th, 1 pt for every other finisher. +3 bonus for beating your season-best on the same course (unless a buoy was missing). +10 bonus after every 10 trials completed. Winner = most points; ties broken by season-best time.
